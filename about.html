// About

Thailand is a personal project of mine that began in January of 2017, when I traveled with my family to Sa Kaeo, Thailand to visit with my Aunt who I had never met. In August of 1979, my family was sponsored to come to the United States after staying in a refugee camp for four years, after escaping Cambodia durning t he Khmer Rouge regime.

My aunt ultimately stayed in Thailand with her husband, where she has remained eversince. Over the years we have made trips, but there are usually many years in between each one. Wanting to document as much as I could, I took pictures, hoping to learn more about her way of life, and to capture moments between family that would otherwise go unnoticed.

I feel it is important to record these memories, and to learn more about my family history alongn teh way. As I continue to visit, this gallery will be updated, and kept as a journal of sorts. This is my family.
